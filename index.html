About Me

Hi! My name is Adam Caragine and I am a passionate and innovative software engineer looking to advance my career in the tech industry. My academic and professional background is a blend of sport and entertainment management and computer science. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Sport and Entertainment management from the University of South Carolina, which equipped me with valuable skills in project management, teamwork, leadership and client relations.

After a few years working in the dynamic and fast-paced sports industry, I realized my passion for technology and decided to alter my career path. I received a Master's degree in Computer Science from Pace University, where I gained a solid foundation in programming, algorithms, and software development principles.

When I'm not immersed in programming, I enjoy a wide range of hobbies and interests. I'm an avid runner (currently training for my first marathon), competitive bowler, golfer, gamer, gym-goer, and sports enthusiast. I also cherish my time with family and friends and especially love playing with my two young nieces!

As I build my software engineering career, I welcome opportunities to connect, collaborate, and contribute to exciting projects in the tech industry.
